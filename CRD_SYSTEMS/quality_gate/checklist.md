# CRD Quality Gate

## Strategy

- Is positioning clear and specific?
- Is this distinguishable from competitors?
- Any generic language present?

---

## System

- Are grid rules defined?
- Is spacing logical or arbitrary?
- Is scale behaviour defined?

---

## Logo

- Does it reduce properly?
- Is construction explained?
- Is it dependent on styling?

---

## Colour

- Is there a strict usage rule?
- Or just a palette?

---

## Typography

- Is hierarchy defined?
- Or just font selection?

---

## Application

- Are rules practical?
- Or just mockups?

---

## Final Check

If removed from visuals, does the system still hold?

If not, system is weak.
