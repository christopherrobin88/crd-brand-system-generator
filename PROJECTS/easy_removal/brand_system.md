01. BRAND CORE

Positioning
Easy Removal is a fast local small-load removal service for everyday clean-ups.

Core message
Small loads cleared quickly.

Functional promise
Easy Removal removes garden refuse, household junk and small clear-out loads using a single 4-cubic-metre truck.

Emotional tone
Simple. Helpful. Direct. Reliable. No drama.

⸻

02. VISUAL STRATEGY

Form direction
Use simple geometric forms.

Primary visual cues:

* Truck load space
* Forward movement
* Cleared space
* Compact service area

Avoid literal piles of rubbish.

Density
Low to medium density.

The identity must read quickly from:

* A moving truck
* A WhatsApp profile image
* A flyer on a community board

Symmetry
Use stable horizontal alignment.

The logo should feel grounded and operational.

Visual tension
Use slight forward motion.

This can come from:

* Angled truck line
* Right-facing arrow cue
* Shifted load shape
* Wordmark weight contrast

Do not use aggressive speed marks.

⸻

03. LOGO SYSTEM

Concept
A compact removal mark built around a small truck, open load area or cleared block.

The logo must communicate:

* Quick removal
* Small loads
* Local service
* Practical reliability

Construction logic
Build the mark from simple vector shapes.

Preferred construction:

* Rectangular truck cab
* Open cage or load box
* One clear directional cue
* Solid wheels
* Strong wordmark lockup

The symbol must work without detailed illustration.

Variants
Required variants:

* Primary horizontal logo
* Stacked logo
* Icon-only mark
* One-colour version
* Reversed version
* WhatsApp profile version
* Truck signage version

Reduction logic
At small sizes, remove detail.

Simplification order:

1. Remove cage line detail
2. Remove secondary truck detail
3. Keep truck silhouette or load-box icon
4. Keep wordmark if space allows
5. Use icon-only mark for profile images

⸻

04. CONSTRUCTION RULES

Grid system
Use an 8-unit grid.

Logo mark construction:

* Cab width: 3 units
* Load area width: 5 units
* Wheel diameter: 1 unit
* Base line: 1 unit above lower edge
* Internal cage lines: minimum 0.25 unit stroke

Wordmark spacing:

* Gap between mark and wordmark: 1 unit
* Wordmark baseline aligns to truck base
* Cap height aligns near top of load area

Alignment
All primary lockups align to a shared horizontal baseline.

The mark must not float above the wordmark.

The word “Easy” must not be visually weaker than “Removal”.

Stroke logic
Use consistent stroke weight.

Stroke ratio:

* Primary outline: 1x
* Internal detail: 0.5x
* Small-size version: remove internal detail

Corners should be slightly rounded.

Avoid sharp industrial corners.

⸻

05. SPACING SYSTEM

Clear space
Minimum clear space equals the height of one wheel.

Apply this around:

* Logo mark
* Full lockup
* Icon-only version

For truck signage, increase clear space to two wheel heights.

Minimum size
Digital:

* Full logo minimum width: 160 px
* Stacked logo minimum width: 120 px
* Icon minimum width: 40 px

Print:

* Full logo minimum width: 45 mm
* Stacked logo minimum width: 35 mm
* Icon minimum width: 12 mm

Vehicle signage:

* Full logo minimum width: 500 mm
* Phone number must be larger than secondary service text

06. COLOUR SYSTEM

Primary
Deep practical green.

Suggested value:

* Removal Green: #5C8165

Use for:

* Primary logo
* CTA blocks
* Truck signage
* Uniform marks
* WhatsApp profile background

Secondary
Muted olive.

Suggested value:

* Local Olive: #86A05C

Use for:

* Secondary panels
* Supporting graphic shapes
* Flyer section dividers
* Small highlights

Accent rules
Use a warm utility accent sparingly.

Suggested value:

* Load Ochre: #C39B46

Use only for:

* Callouts
* Price tags
* “Call / WhatsApp” prompts
* Small directional markers

Neutral colours:

* Charcoal: #202A30
* Blue-grey: #273642
* White: #FFFFFF
* Off-white: #FAFAFA

Rules:

* Logo must work in one colour.
* Green must carry the brand.
* Ochre must not dominate.
* Avoid bright recycling green.
* Avoid black-and-yellow hazard styling.

07. TYPOGRAPHY SYSTEM

Typeface direction
Use a sturdy sans serif.

Recommended direction:

* Humanist or geometric sans serif
* Medium to bold weights
* High legibility
* Open counters
* Clean numerals

Avoid:

* Condensed logistics fonts
* Decorative rounded fonts
* Script fonts
* Overly corporate grotesques

Hierarchy
Primary hierarchy:

1. Business name
2. Phone number
3. Service list
4. Local area cue
5. Supporting details

Flyer hierarchy:

* Headline must state the service outcome
* Phone number must be immediately visible
* Service list must be scannable

Case usage
Use title case for the logo.

Use sentence case for messages and service copy.

Use uppercase only for short labels:

* CALL
* WHATSAPP
* SAME-DAY
* LOCAL

Do not set long service lines in uppercase.

⸻

08. SCALE BEHAVIOUR

Breakpoints
Large applications:

* Truck signage
* Yard boards
* Posters

Use:

* Full horizontal logo
* Large phone number
* Short service list
* Strong green field

Medium applications:

* Flyers
* Invoices
* Website header

Use:

* Full logo
* Clear hierarchy
* Three-service maximum per layout

Small applications:

* WhatsApp profile
* Social media avatar
* Stickers

Use:

* Icon-only mark
* High contrast
* No service list
* No fine cage detail

Simplification logic
Remove information before reducing legibility.

Order:

1. Remove supporting copy
2. Remove secondary graphics
3. Remove service list
4. Remove logo detail
5. Use icon-only mark

Never compress the full logo to force it into a small space.

⸻

09. APPLICATION RULES

WhatsApp quotes
Use plain, direct language.

Preferred structure:

* Greeting
* Load type
* Area
* Price or estimate
* Collection timing
* Confirmation prompt

Brand presence:

* Use icon profile image
* Use green header graphic only if needed
* Do not over-design quote messages

Local flyers
Flyer must answer:

* What do they remove?
* Where do they operate?
* How do I contact them?
* Is this for small loads?

Required flyer elements:

* Logo
* Phone number
* “Garden refuse”
* “Household junk”
* “Yard and garage clear-outs”
* “4-cubic-metre truck”
* Local area cue

Truck signage
Truck signage must prioritise:

1. Easy Removal
2. Phone number
3. Small-load removals
4. Garden refuse and junk

Avoid long copy on moving vehicles.

Yard boards
Use large type.

Maximum message:

* Easy Removal
* Small-load removals
* Garden refuse | Junk | Clear-outs
* Phone number

Facebook community groups
Use a simple branded image.

Do not use heavy promotional layouts.

Use service photos where possible.

Invoices
Use restrained branding.

Logo top left.
Contact details top right.
Green accent line only.

Uniforms
Use one-colour logo.

Left chest placement.
Back print may include phone number.

Website or landing page
Hero message:

* Small-load removals in Cape Town

Primary CTA:

* WhatsApp for a quote

Support copy:

* Garden refuse, household junk and clear-outs.

10. MISUSE RULES

Do not use skip-bin imagery.

Do not use large moving-truck imagery.

Do not make the brand look like a furniture moving company.

Do not use cartoon rubbish piles.

Do not use complex environmental symbols.

Do not use leaves as the primary concept unless secondary and minimal.

Do not use hazard stripes.

Do not use distressed industrial textures.

Do not overstate scale.

Do not imply heavy-duty commercial logistics.

Do not use fine logo detail on WhatsApp avatars.

Do not stretch the truck icon.

Do not place the logo on low-contrast green photography.

Do not make the phone number smaller than secondary service copy.

Do not use more than three colours in one small-format application.
