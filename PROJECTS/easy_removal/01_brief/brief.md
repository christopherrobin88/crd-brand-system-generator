# Easy Removal — Brand Brief

## 01. Business Overview

Easy Removal is a local small-load removal service operating in Cape Town.

Core function:
- Remove unwanted items quickly and reliably using a single 4-cube truck

This is not a large-scale moving company.  
It is a **fast, accessible, everyday removal solution**.

---

## 02. Service Offering

- Garden refuse removal  
- Household junk removal  
- Yard and garage clear-outs  

Capacity:
- 4 cubic metre truck  

Positioning:
- Quick jobs
- Small loads
- Local area focus

---

## 03. Target Audience

Primary:
- Homeowners in suburbs
- People doing clean-ups, garden work, decluttering

Secondary:
- Garden service teams
- Small contractors needing disposal

Behaviour:
- Wants quick, simple solution
- Not price-shopping heavily
- Values responsiveness and ease

---

## 04. Core Problem

Current alternatives:
- Hiring a skip (slow, admin-heavy)
- Doing multiple dump runs (effort)
- Large removal companies (overkill)

Gap:
- No simple, fast, low-friction solution for small loads

---

## 05. Value Proposition

“Send a message. We come. We load. It’s gone.”

Key outcomes:
- No effort
- No logistics
- No delay

---

## 06. Brand Positioning

Easy Removal is:

- Fast
- Simple
- Reliable
- Local

Not:
- Corporate
- Industrial
- Premium
- Eco-preachy

---

## 07. Brand Personality

- Direct
- Friendly
- Practical
- No-nonsense

Tone:
- Human
- Clear
- Confident
- Not clever

---

## 08. Core Message

Primary:
WE LOAD. YOU RELAX.

Supporting:
Fast. Simple. Reliable.

---

## 09. Differentiation

Not cheaper. Not bigger.

Different because:
- Immediate response
- Small-job focused
- Personal service (Nico)

---

## 10. Usage Context

- WhatsApp first contact
- Flyers
- Local area marketing
- Vehicle branding
- Facebook / local groups

This is a **street-level brand**, not a digital-first startup.

---

## 11. Constraints

- Must work in low-attention environments
- Must be readable at distance
- Must feel trustworthy instantly
- Must scale down to WhatsApp profile

---

## 12. Key Insight

People are not buying removal.

They are buying:
“Someone else dealing with this quickly.”
